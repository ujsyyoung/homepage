Biography

I am currently an Associate Professor with the Department of Computer Science, Jiangsu University, Zhenjiang, Jiangsu, China. I received the Ph.D. degree from University of Science and Technology of China, Hefei, Anhui, China, in 2012, supervised by Prof. Lihua Yue. I received the joint Ph.D. degree from City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, China, in 2012, supervised by Dr. Howard Leung.

My research interests include computer vision, computational photography, and deep learning.
