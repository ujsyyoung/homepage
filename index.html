Biography

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate of the Institute for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University, supervised by Dr. Baining Guo Currently, I'm a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia, supervised by Dr. Peng-Shuai Wang, Dr. Yang Liu and Dr. Xin Tong. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree from University of Science and Technology of China in 2018.

My research interest includes efficient 3D convolution and 3D unsupervised learning.
